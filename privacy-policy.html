Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 3, 2026
Short version: GunoPix processes all images directly in your browser. No image or personal data is ever uploaded to any server. We do not store, sell, or share any of your files.

1 Who We Are

GunoPix is a free online image tool created and operated by WebGuno. Our website is accessible at gunopix.netlify.app.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: webguno@gmail.com

2 Information We Do NOT Collect

GunoPix is a client-side browser application. This means:

  • Images you upload are never sent to any server — all processing happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your image files.
  • We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

3 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit GunoPix, our hosting provider (Netlify) may automatically log standard server access data including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring website

This data is collected by Netlify for infrastructure and security purposes. We do not have direct access to this data and do not use it to identify individuals. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

4 Cookies

GunoPix uses only one cookie/local storage item: your selected color theme (dark or light mode). This is stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server.

If you have advertising enabled on this site, Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details on how they use cookies.

5 Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out via aboutads.info.

6 Children's Privacy

GunoPix does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our service is a general-purpose image tool not directed at children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7 Data Security

Since all image processing happens in your browser and no data is transmitted to our servers, the risk of a data breach involving your files is virtually eliminated. Your images never leave your device.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
